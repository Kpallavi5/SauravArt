About

I'am Saurav

Hello! I'm Saurav, a passionate artist from India. I hold a degree in Fine Arts from the College of Arts & Crafts Patna, Bihar. My work primarily explores abstract painting, realistic portraiture, and contemporary sculpture, drawing inspiration from nature, human emotions, and cultural heritage. With a strong foundation in fine arts, I aim to evoke emotions such as the beauty and chaos of nature, cultural diversity and unity, and the struggle for identity and freedom through my creations. Over the years, I have had the privilege of showcasing my work in The Indian Art Fair and the International Exhibition of Painting & Sculpture in ICCR Kolkata, and I continuously seek to push the boundaries of my craft. Each piece I create is a journey, reflecting my deep connection with the pursuit of authenticity and truth, and the exploration of identity and belonging. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope my art resonates with you and inspires new perspectives. .
