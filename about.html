Perticipations & Exhibitions

01.

International Exhibition of Painting & Sculpture At Nandlal Bose Art Gallery,ICCR Kolkata

Participating in the International Exhibition of Painting & Sculpture at Nandlal Bose Art Gallery, ICCR Kolkata, was a significant milestone. I had the privilege to learn and grow artistically, and I was honored to be among the top 10 participants out of 100

02.

Open Art Exhibition Kalastambh at Devlanlikar Kala Vithika, Indore:

Participating in the Open Art Exhibition Kalastambh at Devlanlikar Kala Vithika, Indore, MP, was a rewarding experience. I showcased my artwork, which was greatly appreciated by the attendees. I was honored with a memento and a certificate in recognition of my contributions.

03.

The Indian Art Fair, Delhi

Attending the prestigious Indian Art Fair in Delhi was a transformative experience. With works from artists worldwide, I immersed myself in diverse artistic expressions to learn and grow. This exposure broadened my perspective and inspired me to refine my craft
